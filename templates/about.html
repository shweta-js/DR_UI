About

Technical Excellence Meets Eye Health Welfare

Our website brings together the realms of technology and eye health welfare to offer a powerful tool for the early detection and prediction of retinal diseases.

The primary objective of this website is to provide an accessible and user-friendly platform that empowers individuals to gain insights into their retinal health.

Promoting Early Detection and Diagnosis

Early detection of retinal diseases plays a crucial role in their effective management and treatment. With our advanced prediction system, we aim to contribute to the early identification of conditions such as diabetic retinopathy, cataract, and glaucoma.

By enabling users to upload or capture retinal images, our system leverages state-of-the-art machine learning models to analyze these images and predict potential retinal diseases. We strive to provide users with informative predictions that serve as a valuable tool for initiating early conversations with healthcare professionals and seeking appropriate medical care.
